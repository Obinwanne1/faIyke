# SKILL: job-search

## TRIGGER
"find jobs", "job search", "analyze job", "match this job", "job germany"

## GOAL
Evaluate job fit and surface best opportunities with clear actions.

## PROCESS
1. Extract job requirements
2. Compare against profile
3. Score match (0–100)
4. Identify gaps and strengths
5. Recommend actions

## OUTPUT FORMAT
- **Summary** — role + company snapshot
- **Match Score** — 0–100 with rationale
- **Strengths** — what qualifies
- **Gaps** — what's missing
- **Actions** — next steps to apply or improve fit

## RULES
- Germany market priority
- Salary threshold: €3.5k+/month
- IT roles focus
- No generic advice
