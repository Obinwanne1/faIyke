## GOAL
Evaluate job fit and identify best opportunities.

## PROCESS
1. Extract job requirements
2. Compare with profile
3. Score match
4. Identify gaps
5. Suggest improvements

## OUTPUT FORMAT
- Summary
- Match Score (0–100)
- Strengths
- Gaps
- Actions

## RULES
- Prioritize Germany market
- Consider salary thresholds (€3.5k+/month)
- Focus on relevant IT roles
- Avoid generic suggestions
